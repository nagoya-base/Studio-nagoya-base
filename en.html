Frequently Asked Questions

FAQ

Can I shoot with a smartphone?
Yes. The lighting environment is set up so that you can get good-quality photos even with a smartphone.
Is bondage photography allowed?
Yes. However, sexual acts and shoots involving sexual services are prohibited.
Can I bring my own equipment?
Yes. You may bring your own gear. Standard in-studio equipment and props are included in the rental fee, and owned gear such as strobes is available on prior request.
What is the cancellation policy?
Up to 2 days before use: free / the previous day: 50% of the booking fee / same day: 100% of the booking fee.
What payment methods are available?
Cash, PayPay (on-site), and online credit card payment are available.
Do you offer rope instruction or lessons?
No. We provide the studio space only and do not offer lessons or technical instruction.
Can anyone use the studio?
Yes, regardless of gender, but all users and companions must be 18 or older. High school students are not permitted.
Can I extend my booking on the day?
Only if the next time slot is available. Because same-day communication may not always be possible, please consult us in advance if you think you may need extra time.
How do I receive the key?
Instructions will be provided at the time of booking.
Can I change my booking date?
One date change is accepted free of charge up to the previous day. A second change or later is treated as a cancellation.
Can I lay the background paper on the floor?
Yes, but please avoid using it with shoes or bare feet to keep it clean.
Is there parking?
There is no dedicated parking. Please use a nearby coin-operated parking lot.
Is commercial use allowed?
Yes. Commercial shoots, fan-site content, and paid creative projects are allowed. However, all performers must consent, and all applicable laws and platform rules must be followed.
Can I use a shower?
No. Shower use is not available.
I left something behind.
Lost items are stored for 7 days after discovery. Please contact us by X DM or email as soon as possible. If the item is not claimed after 7 days, it may be reported to the police.

Terms of Use

1. Purpose and Eligibility

This studio is a private space for photography and creative expression. Bondage photography is also available. All users and accompanying persons must be 18 years of age or older, and high school students are not permitted. When necessary, the operator may request age confirmation through social media accounts or similar means.

2. How the Studio May Be Used

  • You may use the studio only during your reserved time slot. Same-day extension is possible only if the next time slot is available.
  • For bondage photography, bookings of 3 hours or longer are recommended.
  • The standard number of users is 1 to 4 people. Use by 5 or more people requires prior approval.
  • Please complete cleaning and pack-up within the reserved time.
  • Please ventilate the room appropriately during use.

3. Prohibited Conduct

  • Sexual acts, sexual services, or pornography production
  • Illegal drugs, intoxicated use, or conduct contrary to public order
  • Eating or drinking inside the studio except hydration from containers with lids
  • Use of flames or dangerous materials
  • Smoking indoors, including electronic cigarettes
  • Unauthorized modification, destruction, or removal of fixtures, equipment, or props
  • Entering or leaving outside the reserved time
  • Interfering with indoor cameras or safety equipment

4. Safety Rules for Suspension and Bondage

  • Users must confirm the safety of fixtures, rope, bamboo, and related items before use.
  • Activities that involve hanging by the neck or restricting breathing are strictly prohibited.
  • Please keep emergency scissors and rescue tools ready and stop immediately if anything unusual happens.

5. Publication and Distribution of Images

Publishing images taken at the studio is allowed when all performers consent, privacy is respected, location-identifying information is not disclosed, and all laws and platform rules are followed.

6. Use of Equipment
