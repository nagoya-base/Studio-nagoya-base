Safety Rules

Please Read Before Booking

  • All users and accompanying guests must be 18 or older.
  • Sexual acts and sexual services are prohibited.
  • Rope suspension must only be performed by experienced users.
  • Suspension use requires at least three people.
  • Suspension from the neck is strictly prohibited.
  • Rope activity after consuming alcohol is prohibited.
  • The studio does not provide rope instruction or technical lessons.
  • Users are responsible for their own safety and consent.
  • Follow all instructions regarding equipment and studio use.

Food and drinks are permitted only around the low table and school desk area. Eating and drinking are prohibited in the tatami area and near the suspension frame. Please take all trash with you.

Alcohol may be brought in, but parties and drinking gatherings are not permitted. Rope activities after drinking alcohol are strictly prohibited.

Please read the full Terms of Use before booking.

    FAQ

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Can I use the studio for rope photography?
    Yes. Rope photography and suspension are welcome. Sexual acts and sexual services are strictly prohibited, and suspension should only be performed by experienced users with at least three people present.
    Can I shoot with a smartphone?
    Yes. The lighting is set up so you can get good results even with a smartphone.
    Is commercial use allowed?
    Yes. Commercial shoots and paid creative projects are allowed, provided all performers consent and applicable laws and platform rules are followed.
    What payment methods are available?
    Cash, PayPay (on-site), and online credit card payment are available.
    Is there parking?
    There is no dedicated parking. Please use a nearby coin-operated parking lot.
