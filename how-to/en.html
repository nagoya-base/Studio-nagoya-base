How to Use

Reservation Flow

Before Your Visit

  1. 1
    Check availability on the calendar Check the calendar on the X profile page or on each room page to confirm your preferred date and time is open.
  2. 2
    Make a reservation via X (DM) or email Please provide your preferred date, time, duration, and room name. We will confirm your booking after reviewing.
  3. 3
    Receive the room number and passcode After your booking is confirmed, we will send you the room number and entry passcode for your visit.
  4. 4
    Review the entry instructions Please check how to locate the key, unlock the door, and turn on the lights before your visit.
    📄 Key location guide (PDF)
    📄 Lock instructions (PDF)
    📄 Switch instructions (PDF)
  5. 5
    Pay the fee (prepayment) Cash: Write your booking name and amount on the envelope, then place it in the payment box inside the room. PayPay: Scan the QR code in the room to pay.
  6. 6
    Start your session Once you are ready, feel free to use the studio freely.
    📄 Tatami mat instructions (PDF)
    📄 Hue lighting instructions (PDF)

After Your Visit

  1. 7
    Return items to their original place Please return all equipment and props to where you found them.
  2. 8
    Quick clean-up Please sweep the floor with the vacuum cleaner or floor wiper provided.
  3. 9
    Turn off A/C and lights Turn off all lights and air conditioning before leaving.
  4. 10
    Lock up and return the key Return the key to its original location, set the dial to 0000, then lock the door as you leave.
