Terms of Use

1. Purpose and Eligibility

This studio is a private space for photography and creative expression. Bondage photography is also available. All users and accompanying persons must be 18 years of age or older, and high school students are not permitted. When necessary, the operator may request age confirmation through social media accounts or similar means.

2. How the Studio May Be Used

  • You may use the studio only during your reserved time slot. Same-day extension is possible only if the next time slot is available.
  • For bondage photography, bookings of 3 hours or longer are recommended.
  • The standard number of users is 1 to 4 people. Use by 5 or more people requires prior approval.
  • Please complete cleaning and pack-up within the reserved time.
  • Please ventilate the room appropriately during use.

3. Prohibited Conduct

  • Bringing in or consuming alcoholic beverages, or entering while intoxicated
  • Sexual acts, sexual services, or pornography production
  • Illegal drugs or other conduct contrary to public order and morality
  • Eating or drinking inside the studio except hydration from containers with lids
  • Generating loud noise or vibration that disturbs neighbors; please be especially mindful of sound levels after 21:00
  • Unauthorized modification, destruction, removal, or unauthorized opening of storage of fixtures, equipment, or props
  • Disposing of personal waste in studio bins, or leaving garbage behind
  • Scattering liquids (water, oil, etc.) on floors or equipment
  • Waiting or loitering in front of the studio entrance
  • Use of flames or dangerous materials
  • Smoking indoors or on the balcony or near ventilation, including electronic cigarettes; please smoke outside the building
  • Entering or leaving outside the reserved time
  • Interfering with, disabling, or obstructing indoor cameras or safety equipment
  • Publishing images of performers without their explicit consent
  • Other conduct deemed inappropriate by the operator

4. Safety Rules for Suspension and Bondage

  • Users must confirm the safety of fixtures, rope, bamboo, and related items before use.
  • Activities that involve hanging by the neck or restricting breathing are strictly prohibited.
  • Please keep emergency scissors and rescue tools ready and stop immediately if anything unusual happens.

5. Publication and Distribution of Images

Publishing or distributing images taken at the studio (including on social media, streaming services, subscription services, paid content, and fan sites) is permitted provided that all of the following conditions are met:

  • All performers have given their explicit consent
  • No third-party privacy or personal information is included
  • No information that identifies the studio location or the operator is disclosed
  • All applicable laws and platform rules are followed
  • No content that constitutes obscene material under applicable law is published or sold

Violation of this section may result in suspension of use, permanent ban from the studio, and a claim for damages.

Sexual acts, sexual services, pornography production, and the filming, production, or sale of content that constitutes obscene material are strictly prohibited.
